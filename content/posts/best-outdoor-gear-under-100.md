## Safety Considerations for Budget Gear

**Know Your Limits:**
- Budget gear works well within reasonable conditions
- Avoid extreme weather or remote locations until experienced
- Always carry backup safety items (extra batteries, emergency shelter)
- Test all gear at home before depending on it outdoors

**Essential Safety Items:**
- First aid kit appropriate for your activity level
- Emergency whistle for signaling help
- Weather-appropriate clothing layers
- Navigation tools (map, compass, GPS)
- Emergency shelter (space blanket minimum)

## Gear Maintenance for Longevity

**Tent Care:**
- Always dry completely before packing
- Clean and treat zippers regularly
- Store in cool, dry location
- Repair small tears immediately

**Backpack Maintenance:**
- Empty and clean after each trip
- Check zippers and buckles for wear
- Store with compartments open for air circulation
- Treat with DWR coating annually

**General Equipment:**
- Clean gear promptly after use
- Inspect for damage before each trip
- Follow manufacturer maintenance guidelines
- Replace worn items before failure
