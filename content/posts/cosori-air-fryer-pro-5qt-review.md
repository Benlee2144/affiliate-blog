## The Honest Downsides

No product is perfect. Here's what actual owners complain about:

### Temperature Runs Hot at Low Settings

TechGearLab found the Pro 5QT runs 12-20°F hotter than displayed at temperatures below 400°F. At 350°F, it's actually cooking at 362-370°F. This means:
- Reduce cooking time by 1-2 minutes for delicate items
- Check food earlier than recipes suggest
- At 400°F+, accuracy is dead-on

### Display Sleeps When Basket Removed

Pull out the basket to shake your fries, and the entire display goes dark. It's mildly annoying but not a dealbreaker—the timer keeps running in the background.

### No Built-In Preheat Cycle

Unlike the Cosori Pro II, this model requires manual preheating. Add 3-5 minutes to your cooking routine if you want optimal results.

## What About the Recall?

In 2023, Cosori recalled approximately 2 million air fryers due to wiring issues. **The Pro 5QT (model B0936FGLQS) was NOT part of that recall.**

The recalled models were older units with different internal wiring. The current Pro 5QT has updated components. You can verify any Cosori product's recall status at [recall.cosori.com](https://recall.cosori.com).
