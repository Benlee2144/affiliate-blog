## The Honest Downsides

### Bluetooth Connectivity Issues

This is the most common complaint. Users report dropouts during walks, with audio cutting out 5-8 times per outing. Reddit threads are full of similar reports.

Potential fixes:
- Forget and re-pair the earbuds
- Disable multipoint connection
- Keep phone in same-side pocket as primary earbud

### Fit Can Be Finicky

Some users struggle to get a proper seal with the included tips. If the seal test in the Sony app fails, sound quality and ANC suffer dramatically.

Solution: Third-party tips like Comply foam or SpinFit often work better than Sony's stock options.

### Case Build Quality

The case lid feels "a bit wobbly" compared to the XM4. It's not a dealbreaker, but noticeable if you've used the previous generation.

### No Multipoint by Default

You can connect to two devices simultaneously, but it's disabled by default and can cause stability issues when enabled.

**Transparency Mode:** "Speak to Chat" and manual transparency provide natural ambient awareness when needed, with customizable levels.
