## The Honest Downsides

### 24-Hour Freeze Time

This is the dealbreaker for many. Your base must freeze solid for 24 hours before processing. No impulse ice cream cravings satisfied here.

The workaround: Keep 4-6 pints in rotation. By the time you eat one, another is ready.

### It's Loud

One Reddit user described it as *"a jet engine taking off in my kitchen for 3 minutes."* If you have sleeping kids or sensitive pets, plan accordingly.

### Recipe Book Is Terrible

The included recipes are "really, really bad." Ignore the manual and head straight to Reddit or TikTok for actually good recipes.

### Limited Pint Containers

Only 2 pints included. You'll need extras immediately. Budget an additional $20-30 for more containers.
