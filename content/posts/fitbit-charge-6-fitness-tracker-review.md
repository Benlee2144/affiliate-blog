## The Honest Downsides

### Fitbit Premium Paywall

Many features (Daily Readiness Score, detailed sleep insights, workout videos) require Fitbit Premium at $10/month. The base experience is good; the full experience costs extra.

### Small Screen Limits Functionality

You won't read emails or scroll social media. Text notifications show partial content. It's a fitness tracker that does some smart things—not a smartwatch.

### Proprietary Charger

Lose the magnetic charger and you're stuck ordering a replacement. No universal USB-C charging here.

### Subscription Fatigue

Between Fitbit Premium and music streaming subscriptions, the monthly costs add up. Consider this ongoing expense.
