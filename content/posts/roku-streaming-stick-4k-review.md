## The Honest Downsides

### Home Screen Advertising

Roku shows promoted content and ads on the home screen. You'll see movie promotions and sponsored tiles. Some users find this annoying on a device they own.

The ads don't interrupt streaming—they're just visual clutter on the interface.

### No Built-In Assistant

Unlike Fire Stick (Alexa) or Chromecast (Google Assistant), Roku's voice features are limited to search and navigation. No smart home control or general questions.

### 4K Requires Good WiFi

Streaming 4K HDR needs consistent 25+ Mbps. If your WiFi is spotty, you'll see buffering or resolution drops.
