## Technical Deep Dive: How TempShield Works

### The Science of Vacuum Insulation

Double-wall construction creates a vacuum between inner and outer walls. Without air molecules to transfer heat, temperature change slows dramatically.

**Why vacuum matters:** Heat transfers through conduction (direct contact), convection (air movement), and radiation (electromagnetic waves). Vacuum eliminates conduction and convection.

**Why cheap bottles fail:** Poor vacuum seals allow air infiltration. Over time, insulation degrades as the vacuum weakens.

### Material Engineering Excellence

**18/8 Stainless Steel** = 18% chromium, 8% nickel. This specific alloy provides:
- Corrosion resistance (won't rust or pit)
- Flavor neutrality (no metallic taste)
- Durability under daily use
- Recyclability at end of life

**Pro-Grade Powder Coating** resists chips better than paint. When it does chip, the steel underneath won't rust like aluminum alternatives.

### The Wide Mouth Advantage

Beyond ice cube convenience, the wide opening enables:
- Better mixing for protein shakes
- Easier cleaning access
- Interchangeable lid systems
- Visual confirmation of contents

Most narrow-mouth bottles sacrifice functionality for perceived aesthetics. Hydro Flask chose function first.
