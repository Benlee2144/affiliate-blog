## Maintenance and Care Guide

### Daily Maintenance (30 seconds)

1. Rinse brush head thoroughly after use
2. Shake out excess water from handle
3. Store upright in ventilated area
4. Wipe charging base weekly with damp cloth

### Weekly Deep Cleaning (5 minutes)

1. Remove brush head and rinse connection point
2. Clean handle with antibacterial wipe (avoid charging port)
3. Soak brush head in hydrogen peroxide 5 minutes
4. Check charging contacts for buildup or corrosion

### Monthly Care Routine

1. Deep clean charging base with soft brush and mild soap
2. Inspect brush head wear (replace if bristles frayed)
3. Update app software and sync usage data
4. Check battery performance and charging times

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

**Brush won't charge:** Clean charging contacts, try different outlet
**Battery drains quickly:** Recalibrate by full discharge then full charge
**Pressure sensor inconsistent:** Reset brush settings in app
**App won't connect:** Clear Bluetooth cache, re-pair device
