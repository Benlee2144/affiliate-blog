## The Honest Downsides

### It's Loud

There's no sugarcoating this: *"Ear bleeding loud on higher settings."* The power that crushes ice also creates significant noise. Morning smoothies will wake the household.

### 48oz Container May Feel Small

Compared to 64oz Vitamix models, the E310's container limits batch sizes. Fine for most uses, but something to consider for entertaining or meal prep.

### No Presets

Unlike higher-end Vitamix models, there are no automatic blend cycles. You control speed manually throughout.

### Lid Seal Quirks

Some users report the lid requires precise positioning to seal properly, and occasional leaking during cleaning.
