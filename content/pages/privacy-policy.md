## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **IP Address**: For geographic analytics and spam prevention

### Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Analytics**: To understand how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics)
- **Preferences**: To remember your theme preference (light/dark mode)
- **Advertising**: Third-party advertising partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Improve our content and user experience
- Analyze traffic patterns and site performance
- Display relevant advertisements
- Prevent spam and abuse

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information about your visits. You can opt out using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Amazon Associates Program

We participate in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click an affiliate link, Amazon may place cookies on your browser to track referrals. This is governed by [Amazon's Privacy Policy](https://www.amazon.com/privacy).

### Google AdSense

We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Object**: Object to processing of your personal data

For GDPR/CCPA requests, contact us at [your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com).
